    Flame Tongue
    Planet: Sudaten
    Terrain: Prarie
    Time: Day
    45.44 degrees
    Default Mech: Nova

    Objectives:
    Primary: Destroy Pumping Station & HQ.

    Blade Splint

    Planet: Zoetermeer
    Terrain: Sparse Urban
    Time: Dusk
    48.43 degrees
    Default Mech: Jenner
    Max Tonnage: 75
    Objectives:
    Primary: Recon Power Converter & Destroy if Active
    Defenders: 3 Kit Foxes
    First Intersection in road you come toTurn Right
    The power converter is on the left behind the buildings
    Follow road until you can hit E and target the first Kit Fox
    Make a wide left arc around the Kit Foxes so you can get visual of the power converter.
    Line it up so that the Kit Foxes are on the other side of the power converter
    when you advance to it. (Don't forget to lock on it and identify it as active or you will not get credit for it)
    They will try to fire on you hitting the converter whilst you hammer the other side.
    When it is done run, don't walk, to the dust off site doing as much damage
    as you can to the kit foxes and using your jump jets, remember this is a hit and run.

    Sable Flame

    Planet: MacDuff
    Terrain: Cratered/Vacuum
    Time: Night
    -154.45 degrees
    Default Mech: Timber Wolf
    Max Tonnage: 55
    Objectives:
    Primary: Capture Sensor Array Dome
    Secondary: Destroy all Defending Mechs
    Tertiary: Destroy Targets of opportunity
    Primary Objective:
    Highlight Nav Eta
    Save your LRMs for later (put them in the second group)
    Autopilot until you hear enemy powerup detected
    wait till they go left then you go right (come up behind them)
    use the dome to split them up (they won't shoot at it)
    take out the mad dogs by shooting the legs like crazy
    take out the hellbringers left arm first (PPC) then waste him.
    Hellbringers have soft torsos.
    after this more powerups far West of the Dome.
    West Powerups:
    1 Mad Dog
    1 Hellbringer
    Don't go too far from Dome stay West of it but not far from it.
    from a distance use LLaser(even if not target or reded for range)
    shoot at hellbringer (use LRMS when they get in range)
    Leg shoot the Mad Dog
    Likely you should really hurt them so they'll run away.
    They will likely go further west to draw you away from
    the dome.
    The Last Powerup
    When you hear another Enemy Powerup Detected
    Run to the East of Dome
    1 Hellbringer
    1 Mad Dog
    will show up.
    Try to shoot at both of them from a distance to draw
    their attention away from the dome.
    LLaser the legs of the Mad Dog and Torso the Hellbringer.
    When you finish them off the straggling mechs will
    Attack (those that you ran off, if you didn't kill'em)
    They should be easy targets, just finish them off and
    that is the Secondary Objective complete
    Tertiary Objective:
    The object of opportunity is a JF Dropship NNE of the Dome
    It is nestled in a large crater. The way to do this is the old
    Round-e-Round, just run around the thing full speed while
    hammering it with everything you've got. It will take a while.
    The only thing that it has that will hit you is LRMs (they may
    be SRMs)
    Burning Chrome
    Planet: Evciler
    Terrain: Ice Rift
    Time: Day
    -30.34 degrees
    Default Mech: Storm Crow
    Max Tonnage: 65 (2 mech limit)
    Objectives:
    Primary: Destroy Solar Array at Nav Tau
    Secondary: Destroy all enemy units
    Tertiary: Destroy Targets of opportunity
    Dustoff: Nav Upsilon
    First Contact: to your Right when going down the canyon. 2 Kitfoxes Use your Large Lasers on them (easy targets)
    After you take out the Kit foxes, continue straight.  You will find a Mad Dog around the bend and behind it a Dropship.
    Take out the MadDog and leave the dropship.  Stay away from the dropship for now.  Turn around and go back
    to where the Kitfoxes were.  Turn right and you'll see some enemy turrets (shoot them by targeting the closest and
    slowly walk toward it until you get lock (red target reticle) then take it out.) you'll hear a powerup
    (1 summoner just around the bend) wait for him to come around the corner and take him out (group fire lasers at torso
    and watch your heat!)  Stay far enough from the dropship to keep out of its PPC range.
    This is the fun part.  Destroying the drop ship.  Using your large lasers wait until you get into range enough to hit it.
    Generally you'll still be out of its range for its Large lasers but within PPC and LRM range.  You must torso twist and run
    full speed toward the canyon wall whilst shooting the dropship.  It will shoot LRMS and PPCs at you but they will (if
    your engines aren't damaged) always fly behind you.  You will have to turn around 3 times or so while hammering the
    dropship to finally destroy it.  (This is the target of opportunity)  Now the easy work.  When your done with the drop
    ship go get the rest of the turrets using the method I wrote above (creep up don't run) which is primarily designed for
    medium lasers (large laser work great). when they are all gone just go up the platform and destroy the Solar array.

    Scortching Sand

    Planet: Twycross
    Terrain: Mesa Desert
    Time: Dawn
    69.56 degrees
    Default Mech: Nova
    Max Tonnage: 70
    Objectives:
    Primary: Capture & Secure Enemy Command Vehicle
    Secondary: Destroy All Defending Units
    Tertiary: Destroy Targets of Opportunity
    2 Jenners on Mesa to the Left of startup (easy targets take out their missle racks)
    Nav Alpha: Storm Crow (take out legs)
    Nav Beta: 2 Elementals (distance PPCs work good)
    Follow navs and you'll finally get enemy target of a turret
    5 Turrets (use lasers or ppcs from a distance)
    2 vehicles (just hammer them)
    After you destroy the vehicles and turrets a enemy powerup will occur
    1 Rifleman will show up at the top of the Mesa across from the command vehicle
    Target him and walk towards the mesa just close enough so that you can still hit him
    when he drops off the cliff, when he does, shoot his legs with everything you have.
    If your good you can take him out before he hits the ground.
