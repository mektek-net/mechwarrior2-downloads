Mechwarrior 2 Cheat Codes

hold CTRL-ALT-SHIFT down while typing codes
    BLORB - Invulnerability
    CIA - Unlimited Ammo
    COLDMISER - Heat Tracking off
    DORCS - Shows the programmers
    ENOLAGAY - Nuke the targeted Mech
    GANKEM - Destroy the Targeted Mech
    HANGAROUND - Hang Around after Mission is Over
    ICANTHACKIT - End Mission
    LAIRDO - Command Other Mech/Vehicles/Dropships
    MEEPMEEP - Time Compression Key Enabled
    MICHELIN - See Bounding Spheres
    MIGHTYMOUSE - Unlimited Jump Jets
    TINKERBELL - Free Floating Camera (C to Disable)
    TLOFRONT - Rear View Camera becomes Front View Camera
    UNMEEPMEEP - Time Compression Off
    XRAY - Xray Vision (W to Disable)
    ZMAK - Time Expansion Enabled

    Clan of the Ghost Bear Cheat Codes

    hold CTRL-ALT-SHIFT down while typing codes
    DORCS - Shows the programmers
    CLARK - Xray Vision
    KENT - Invulnerability
    KABOOM - Nukes Everything (use KENT before trying)
    PALEX - Nuke Current Target
    PUTZ - Force Completion of Mission
    TIK - See Bounding Spheres
    SPEEDYGONZALEZ - Enable time compression key
    BURR - Heat Tracking off
    JUMBO - Gives you Jump Jets

    Mechwarrior 2: Mercenaries

    hold CTRL-ALT-SHIFT down while typing codes
    ANTIJOLT - Time expansion enabled
    BEHOLDMYGLORY - Free-eye mode
    BUBBLEBOY - Display bounding spheres
    CRAZYSEXYCOOL - Infinite jump-juice
    INMYBEAUTIFULBALLOON - Gives you Jump Jets
    ITSDABOOOMB - Nukes targeted mech
    ISEENFIREANDISEENRAIN - Unlimited ammo
    LIKETHECOMSTARBABY - Automatically succeed in mission (it doesn't set your mission goals as successful though)
    ONTIMEEVERYWHERE - Time compression key enabled
    OOOHHHLLLAAALLLAAA - Disable heat tracking
    REDJACKANDTIKRULES - Destroy targeted mech without the splash damage of "itsdabooomb" code
    SUPERFUNKICALIFRAGISEXY - Invulnerbility
    UNDFLASHYFLASHY - AutoGrouping enabled (makes all weapons fire from the first trigger)
    WALKTHISWAY - Leading Recticle enabled (makes enemies follow your targeting crosshairs towards you)
    WEDIDITAGAIN - Shows the programmers
